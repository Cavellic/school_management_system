Subjects

Below are the subjects that we offer as Cornerstone College Westgate Harare.

NB-Note that we offer Cambridge subjects as well

IGCSE Subjects

All pupils take a minimum of 8 (eight) subjects at IGCSE.

Compulsory subjects are:

  1. Mathematics
  2. Combined Science
  3. English
In addition to these, a student is allowed to study the following subjects;
  • Accounts
  • Computers
  • Geography
  • History
  • Heritage
  • Shona
  • BES
  • Chemisty
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • Family and Religious Education

A Level Subjects

Subject choices are determined by IGCSE results. All pupils take a minimum of 3 (three) subjects at A Level.

Compulsory subject is AS English Language.

    4. Literature

    Commercials

    Sciences

    4. Biology
